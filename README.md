# LLM Consistency Survey

A thorough literature survey on consistency, reliability, and robustness issues in large language models.

**Live site**: https://johnplake.github.io/llm-consistency-survey/

## What This Covers

- Factual Consistency & Hallucination
- Self-Consistency & Contradiction
- Reasoning Consistency
- Behavioral Consistency & Sycophancy
- Calibration & Uncertainty
- Cross-Context & Format Sensitivity
- Benchmark Reliability
